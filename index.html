FAQ

Questions We Get Asked

How long does a typical project take?+
Project timelines vary by scope. A 5-page web presence takes 2–3 weeks. A full SaaS platform like Cook With Fire is delivered in phased sprints — typically 8–16 weeks for an MVP. We always provide a clear roadmap before we begin.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises?+
Both. Our Starter plan is designed for early-stage businesses and solo founders. Our Enterprise tier serves large organizations and multi-location businesses. We scale our process to match where you are.
Can you handle Arabic and RTL support?+
Yes — Arabic RTL support is a first-class feature in everything we build. We use IBM Plex Sans Arabic for typography and architect our layouts to handle bidirectional content natively from day one, not as an afterthought.
What AI technologies do you work with?+
We work with OpenAI GPT-4o, Gemini Pro, Claude, and open-source LLMs. We build custom RAG pipelines, AI copilots, intelligent automation systems, and ML-powered analytics. Every AI feature is production-grade, not a prototype.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch?+
All plans include post-launch support. Starter includes 30 days, Business includes continuous 24/7 support as part of the retainer, and Enterprise includes SLA-backed guarantees. You always have direct access to the team that built your product.
How do you handle data security and privacy?+
Security is built in from architecture, never bolted on. We implement row-level security (RLS), JWT authentication, encrypted data storage, and regular security audits. All products comply with regional data protection requirements.
Can Cook With Fire integrate with our existing POS?+
Cook With Fire has a robust API layer that supports integrations with major POS systems, payment gateways, and third-party delivery platforms. We also build custom integrations for enterprise clients with specific systems in place.
Do you serve clients outside Qatar?+
Yes — we serve clients across Qatar, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman. We operate as a remote-first team, with project management, design reviews, and development all handled digitally without timezone friction.
What makes AFRA DIGITAL different from other agencies?+
Three things: we build AI-native products (not just websites), we have deep GCC market knowledge (Arabic RTL, local payments, Gulf business culture), and we operate with founder-level accountability — we treat your project as if we co-own the outcome.
How do we get started?+
Fill out the contact form below or WhatsApp us directly. We'll schedule a 30-minute discovery call, assess your project scope, and deliver a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing within 48 hours. No commitment required.
